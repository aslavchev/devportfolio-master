About Me

Hi, I'm Aleksandar Slavchev — a seasoned Quality Assurance Engineer with over 17 years of hands-on experience spanning global tech firms and agile startups.


My passion lies in ensuring robust, user-friendly software, and I’ve contributed to diverse domains including cloud computing, self-driving car apps, financial media, and sports performance platforms.


Throughout my career at companies like VMware and Tumba Solutions, I’ve led QA initiatives, mentored new engineers, and collaborated across international teams to deliver high-quality digital products.

I bring deep expertise in test design, automation (Selenium + Java), and mobile/web QA — all driven by a strong commitment to precision and customer satisfaction.


Outside of tech, I co-founded a sports performance startup, coach youth football, and actively engage in disciplines like trading, language learning, and martial arts.


I’m a firm believer in lifelong learning, discipline, and the power of diverse passions to fuel innovation.
